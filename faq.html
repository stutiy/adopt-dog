Pet-Adoption FAQs

Adopting a pet is one of the most exciting things you can do. Taking responsibility for an animal raises a slew of questions: Will I be a good pet parent? Do I have enough time? What if I’m horrible at training? While there is certainly some anxiety that comes along with adopting a pet, for most people, the rewards far outweigh the risk.

Thank you for using Adopt! We’re here to help you every step of the way from “just looking” at over 250,000 adoptable pets, to bringing your pet home, to living a long and happy life with your new family member.

The following links will take you to some of the most frequently asked questions about adopting a pet that you see on Adopt. If you can’t find the answer below, please contact us (see the bottom of this page) with any additional questions you have.
